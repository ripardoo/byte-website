About Byte

Byte is a curated program that brings exceptional young builders from Finland to Silicon Valley for eight days of focused, high-quality conversations with founders and operators.

Participants are selected for their drive, technical ability, and ambition. The format is intentionally small (eight people) so every meeting is personal and high-signal for both sides.

This is our second cohort. Learn more at joinbyte.so.
